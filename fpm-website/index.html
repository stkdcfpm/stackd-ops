What We Do

End-to-End Trade Services

From finding the right manufacturer to delivering goods to your door, we manage every step of the international trade process.

🔍

Global Sourcing

We identify, vet, and negotiate with manufacturers across China and key global markets — so you get quality goods at the right price.

  • Manufacturer identification & due diligence
  • Sample coordination & QC oversight
  • Price negotiation & contract terms
  • Certificate & compliance verification
🚢

Freight & Shipping

We arrange sea freight for full container loads (FCL) and less-than-container loads (LCL), with door-to-port and door-to-door options.

  • FCL (20GP / 40HQ) and LCL booking
  • Bill of lading & customs documentation
  • Shipment tracking & status updates
  • Dangerous goods (DG) coordination
📋

Procurement Management

We handle the full purchase-order lifecycle — from issuance through to goods receipt — managing supplier relationships on your behalf.

  • Purchase order creation & tracking
  • Supplier payment coordination
  • Lead time management
  • Proforma & commercial invoice handling
📦

Caribbean Market Supply

With deep experience supplying the Caribbean market, we understand local import requirements, duty structures, and delivery expectations.

  • Barbados & wider Caribbean delivery
  • Import duty & landed cost calculations
  • Commercial documentation for customs
  • Local market knowledge & guidance
The Process

How We Work

A straightforward process from initial enquiry to goods delivered.

1

Enquiry

Tell us what you need — product type, quantity, destination, and timeline. We'll assess feasibility and costs.

2

Sourcing

We identify the right suppliers, verify credentials, and negotiate competitive pricing and terms on your behalf.

3

Quotation

You receive a detailed landed-cost quote covering goods, freight, insurance, duties, and our fee.

4

Order & Ship

We place the PO, manage production, arrange freight, and keep you updated throughout transit.

5

Delivered

Goods arrive with full documentation. We handle any post-delivery queries or adjustments.

About Us

A Trade Partner You Can Rely On

FPM International Ltd is a Brighton-based trade intermediary helping businesses source commercial goods from global manufacturers and get them delivered to Caribbean and international markets.

We bridge the gap between buyers and overseas suppliers — handling sourcing, procurement, freight logistics, and documentation so our clients can focus on their core business rather than navigating complex international trade.

With direct supplier relationships across China's key manufacturing regions and established freight networks into the Caribbean, we deliver goods efficiently, transparently, and at competitive landed costs.

We operate with full transparency — every quote includes a detailed breakdown of goods cost, freight, insurance, duties, and our fee. No hidden costs.
