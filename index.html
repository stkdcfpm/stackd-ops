BULK IMPORT
Two ways to import. Option 1 is recommended — no file export needed.
⚡ Option 1 — Import Directly from Google Sheets

Reads directly from your master template in Google Drive. No export, no file, no formatting issues.
File: My Drive > FPM > FPM STACKD - OPS TEMPLATES > stackd-master-template-v1.0.4

Option 2 — CSV Upload (use if data is not in Google Drive)
1Suppliers

Upload your supplier list first. Line items and POs reference suppliers by name — suppliers must exist before you import anything else.

2Line Items

Upload your product catalogue. Supplier Name in the CSV must match exactly what you entered in Step 1.

3Invoices

Upload your existing invoices. Line items are not required — invoices appear on the dashboard immediately with your provided totals. Enrich with line items later by editing each invoice. Status values are case-insensitive: paid, Paid, PAID all work.

Minimum required columns: Invoice #, Buyer, Date, Status, Currency, Grand Total
Optional but recommended: Destination, Buyer Deposit, COGS, Freight Type, POL, POD, COO, Notes
4Purchase Orders

Upload existing POs. Supplier Name must match your supplier list. Invoice # links the PO to an existing invoice — leave blank for standalone POs.
